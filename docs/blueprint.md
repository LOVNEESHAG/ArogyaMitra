# **App Name**: Sehat Dih Village

## Core Features:

- Video Consultation: Enable patients to schedule and join video consultations with doctors.
- Digital Health Records: Securely store and manage patient health records, accessible offline.
- Pharmacy Stock Display: Show medicine availability in local pharmacies via real-time updates.
- Multilingual Support: Offer a UI that supports Punjabi, Hindi, and English.
- Appointment Management: Allow patients to book appointments and receive notifications.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Raisin black (#1A181B) will suggest reliability and trust, crucial in healthcare. It also avoids clich√©s.
- Background color: Floral white (#FFF8F0) will ensure readability and reduce eye strain, fitting for prolonged use.
- Accent color: Erin (#05F140) will suggest health and well-being, creating a calming user experience.
- Accent color 2: Jasmine (#F4D06F) as colorful but dull parts.
- Font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif suitable for headlines or body text.
- Use clear, universally recognizable icons to aid users with limited digital literacy.
- Design a simple, intuitive layout prioritizing ease of navigation on smaller screens. Keep the layout modern and icon/glyph rich.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to provide feedback without overwhelming users.
